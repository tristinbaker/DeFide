# Changelog

## [1.1.0] - 2026-03-25

### Added
- Bible reading progress: pressing Next on a chapter marks it as read; the chapter selector shows filled chips for completed chapters
- Long-press a read chapter chip to unmark it; overflow menu to reset all progress for a book
- Bible streak and Rosary streak widgets on the Home screen
- Bible streak goal setting (1–10 chapters per day) in Settings
- How to Use guide in Settings

### Fixed
- Removed debug test notification button from Settings

## [1.0.0] - 2026-03-25

### Added
- Bible reader with four translations: Douay-Rheims 1899 American Edition, World English Bible (Catholic), Latin Vulgate, and Latin Vulgate (English Translation)
- Full-text search across all Bible translations
- Verse bookmarks and highlights with color picker
- Guided Rosary sessions for all four mysteries (Joyful, Sorrowful, Glorious, Luminous) with bead indicator and scripture references
- Traditional Catholic prayers with tag-based browsing and search
- Novena tracker with progress persistence across sessions
- Seven novenas included: Sacred Heart of Jesus, St. Joseph, 33-Day Marian Consecration (St. Louis de Montfort), Holy Spirit, St. Thérèse of Lisieux, St. Anthony of Padua, Our Lady of Guadalupe
- Home screen with Verse of the Day and today's suggested Rosary mystery
- Link to the Catechism of the Catholic Church on the USCCB website
- Settings screen with theme and translation preferences
- Fully offline — no network permission required
- No analytics, no accounts, no data collection
